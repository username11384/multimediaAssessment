My Dress-Up Darling

Plot

Be aware that this page about the plot contains major spoilers about the anime and some manga content. Click the button to reveal spoilers

My Dress-Up Darling is about an unusual relationship between two people who basically live in opposite worlds. Gojo Wakana, who is an antisocial and insecure doll maker, meets Marin Kitagawa, the popular classmate who is known by everyone. They meet after class, when Gojo is cleaning up, and Marin talks to him how he has to speak up for himself. Gojo then thinks about what Marin had told him His sewing machine stopped working so he used the school one after class. Marin enters the room and sees him there, but instead of her laughing, she admired what he was doing. She is into anime and cosplaying, and asked Gojo to make her a cosplay. He reluctantly agrees, but he isn't sure if he can do a good job.

Their relationship escalates very quickly from here. They get closer and both of them slowly develop romantic feelings for each other. After Gojo finishes her first cosplay of Shizuku-tan, she tries it and it's just perfect for her. This leads to them being together more often, and Gojo helping make her more cosplays.

After the story progresses some more, they are on a phone call, and right after Gojo went to sleep, Marin finally confesses her love to him, which he doesn't realize for now. So far, the manga has covered enough material for a season 2 of the anime.
